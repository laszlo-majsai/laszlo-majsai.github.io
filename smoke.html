The Project

This concept is made for a course called “Designing positive technology tools”. The goal of the class was to choose a mental health issue and design an app or any digital platform, that can help people deal with it. We have chosen addiction.

After several brainstormings, we decided to create an app that helps people quit smoking. It is a concept, that has many already implemented examples for, but we made our own approach to it, that differs from other similar items on the market.
