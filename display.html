The Project

For this assignment we were given a phenomena of nature which inspires us to design some kind of display. Mine was water. At first we had to make sketches and start looking for different shapes that can be developed to a concept of a product. Then we had to make 5 rough design based on those sketches. We had to develop one of those to a finished concept.

The Result

I ended up with this raindrop inspired minimalistic display, which can be hanged in a living room and put some plants, photos or other decorative items in it.
