ABOUT ME

I am a 26 years old Interaction Design MA student at Moholy-Nagy University of Art and Design in Budapest, Hungary. In this programme, we are studying all the key fundementals of creating digital services, from research to create a high-fidelity prototype.

I graduated as a industrial design engineer for my bachelors. It is a key element for this profession to be able to make a connection between the customer and our product. I think interaction design has quite the same intentions as well, just in a different space. However, it is pretty obvious in my opinion, that the future holds the most opportunities in this field of expertise.

I switched to interaction design, because in the digital world you can be creative with almost no limits. Product designers have to face many factors which force them to differ from their original concepts. With digital technology there are less and less boundaries and more opportunities. However, it also means there is always something new to the people to keep up with, and that is why I think interaction design is important. I want to keep people connected with the constantly developing technology, to make them see the opportunities in it instead of the concerns.
