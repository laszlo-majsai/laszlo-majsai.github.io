ABOUT ME

I am a 26 years old Interaction Design MA student at Moholy-Nagy University of Art and Design in Budapest, Hungary. In this programme, we are studying all the key fundementals of creating digital services, from research to create a high-fidelity prototype.

I graduated as a industrial design engineer for my bachelors. It is a key element for this profession to be able to make a connection between the costumer and our product. I think interaction design has quite the same intentions as well, just in a different space. However, it is pretty obvious in my opinion, that the future holds the most opportunities in the digital world.

I switched to interaction design, because in the digital world you can be creative with almost no limits. Product designers have to face many factors which force them to differ from their original concepts. With digital technology there are less and less boundaries and more opportunities. However, it also means there is always something new to the people to keep up with, and that is why I think interaction design is important. I want to keep people connected with the constantly developing technology, to make them see the opportunities in them instead of the concerns.

Product design Excercise

For this assignment we were given a phenomena of nature which inspires us to design some kind of display. Mine was water. At first we had to make sketches and start looking for different shapes that can be developed to a concept of a product. Then we had to make 5 rough design based on those sketches. We had to develop one of those to a finished concept. I ended up with this raindrop inspired minimalistic display, which can be hanged in a living room and put some plants, photos or other decorative items in it.

I was in a group of three, and our project was to design a tool or a system for the university’s new building, which gathering, storing and displaying sensor data. The building was made as a hybrid learning environment, where students are working on projects together using innovative technologies.

Data Visualization

This project was made for a one week long course at my university. In this programme, we studied the latest and most experimental methods of data visualization. At the end of the week, we had to present our own concept. We were allowed to use any source of data, and any form of visualization.

Smoke Quitting App

This concept is made for a course called “Designing positive technology tools”. The goal of the class was to choose a mental health issue and design an app or any digital platform, that can help people deal with it. We have chosen addiction. After several brainstormings, we decided to create an app that helps people quit smoking. It is a concept, that has many already implemented examples for, but we made our own approach to it, that differs from other similar items on the market.

Newsplatform App

This was our biggest project during last semester. We were given four issues, that are common in the rural area of Hungary, and we had to make a concept of a digital tool, that can help solving it. I chose misinformation for my intervention area. I started to investigate, how I, as a designer can help to improve access to reliable information in rural communities to combat fake and biased news sources and strengthen civic participation.
